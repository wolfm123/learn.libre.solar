# Production

A printed circuit board (PCB) is a carrier for electronic components. It serves for mechanical fastening and electrical connection. Almost every electronic device contains one or more printed circuit boards. When a printed circuit board is assembled with electric parts, the term PCBA is used.

Further information can be found in the following Wikipedia Article.

The two components of PCB and electric parts must be combined together to get a working board. This combination process is shown in the following chapters where a MPPT solarcharger is taken as a production example; a PCB for running an electrical solar power system. The production of the PCB without the electrical parts is not part of this guide, it is obtained externally from the manufacturer.

The production process of PCBs will be explained in the following chapters.
